About Zeshaan

With over a decade of experience in New York City's competitive luxury real estate market, Zeshaan Qadir has established himself as a trusted expert in high-end properties across Brooklyn and Manhattan.

Currently a Licensed Real Estate Salesperson at Casa Blanca Real Estate and a designated StreetEasy Expert, Zeshaan has built his reputation on a foundation of integrity, market insight, and technological innovation. His career includes notable tenures at prestigious firms such as Brown Harris Stevens (BHS) and Spire Group, where he generated millions in sales and achieved impressive revenue increases through innovative strategies and client-focused service.

Zeshaan's unique blend of real estate expertise and mortgage business development knowledge allows him to offer comprehensive guidance to clients, helping them navigate the complexities of New York's luxury market with confidence and clarity.
